Our Story

Established in 2005, Spice Haven has been a culinary landmark in Mumbai, bringing the authentic flavors of India to food lovers. Our journey began with a simple vision: to serve traditional Indian cuisine with a modern twist while maintaining the rich heritage of our culinary traditions.

Our master chefs, with decades of experience in royal kitchens and five-star hotels, create dishes that tell stories of India's diverse culinary landscape. From the royal kitchens of Rajasthan to the coastal flavors of Kerala, we bring you the best of Indian cuisine under one roof.
